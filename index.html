03 / Current Research

Spatial Architecture Simulation

My current work focuses on simulation infrastructure for spatial architectures. I am interested in representing mapped programs and studying their execution under different architectural configurations.

The project includes cycle-level modeling, validation, and tools for inspecting program mappings and runtime behavior.
